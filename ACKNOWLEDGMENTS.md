# Acknowledgments

The Astral library builds upon the work of many astronomers, mathematicians, and software developers who have contributed to the field of computational astronomy.

## Primary Sources

### Astronomical Algorithms

- **Jean Meeus** - "Astronomical Algorithms" (2nd Edition, 1998)
  - The definitive reference for astronomical calculations
  - Provided the mathematical foundations for solar and lunar calculations
  - Algorithms for sun position, moon phases, and coordinate transformations

- **US Naval Observatory** - "Astronomical Almanac"
  - Annual publication providing reference astronomical data
  - Validation source for calculation accuracy
  - Standard reference for astronomical constants

- **Chapront-Touz√©, M. & Chapront, J.** - "ELP-2000 lunar series"
  - High-precision lunar ephemeris
  - Foundation for accurate lunar position calculations
  - Used in simplified form for moonrise/moonset calculations
